Cookie Policy

Welcome to our website's Cookie Policy. This page explains how we use cookies on our website.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer or mobile device) when you browse websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes including to analyze website traffic, improve user experience, and deliver personalized content. We may also use cookies from third-party services such as analytics or advertising providers.

Types of Cookies We Use

We may use several types of cookies on our website, including:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Functionality cookies: Used to enhance user experience by remembering preferences.
  • Advertising cookies: Used to deliver targeted advertisements based on user interests.

    • Managing Cookies

    You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.
